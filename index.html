About Me

My interest in computers started when I was around nine years old, after building my first PC and getting my first internet connection. From that point, I became curious about how systems worked, how networks connected devices, and what was happening behind the screen.

I spent a lot of time searching for technical material, experimenting, breaking things, fixing them, and learning how different parts of a computer and network fit together. That curiosity developed into a serious interest in infrastructure, networking, security, and troubleshooting.
