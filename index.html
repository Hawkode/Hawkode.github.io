IT Asset Inventory and Site Tracking System using Snipe-IT

Aug 2025 – Sep 2025

The goal was to improve how IT equipment is tracked across multiple sites and make it easier to manage assets, accessories, users, and physical desk locations.

I populated IT asset records across multiple sites, created a site-based naming standard, updated asset statuses such as Ready to Deploy, Pending, and Decommissioned, and added accessories including monitors, keyboards, mice, headsets, docking stations, cameras, and other peripherals.

I also created user records, assigned assets and accessories to users, organised equipment by site and location, created location maps, and labelled desks with numbers to improve physical asset tracking.

This improved visibility of available equipment, assigned devices, accessory stock, and desk-level location tracking. It also supports onboarding, offboarding, troubleshooting, audits, and hardware lifecycle management.

Snipe-IT • Asset Management • Site Tracking • Inventory • Lifecycle Management

Network Cabling & Port Mapping

The aim was to create a clearer link between server room patch panel ports and wall sockets, making troubleshooting and future changes easier to manage.

I traced network cables from patch panels to wall sockets, matched wall socket ports with corresponding patch panel ports, applied clear numbering labels, and documented port-to-location records.

This improved visibility of network access points by site and location and created a more reliable reference for troubleshooting, hardware setup, and office changes.

Accurate labelling helps with faster troubleshooting, cleaner hardware deployments, easier office moves, better asset tracking, and less guesswork.

This project also gave me more hands-on experience with structured cabling, patch panels, physical network layouts, and the practical side of maintaining site infrastructure.

Networking • Structured Cabling • Patch Panels • Port Mapping • Site Documentation
